    • Rules

    1. Do not cheat.

    Cheating can be defined as, but is not limited to the following:

    • X-ray (including texture packs)
    • Hacking
    • Abusing exploits
    • Alt abuse (see rule #19)

    2. Do not flood chat/advertise/post shortened links.

    • Chat spam (character and repeat messages) should be handled by the chat filter. Attempting to bypass this is not allowed.
    • Advertising: this includes other MC servers as well as personal Discord servers etc. Advertising your twitch stream is fine as long as you are currently playing on Plasma Network, and you limit your in-game ads to 1 per 5 minutes.
    • Avoid posting shortened links in chat, and keep any links appropriate.

    3. No scamming.

    Do not intentionally scam other players. This includes but is not limited to:

    • Breaking an agreement regarding items sold/traded.
    • Selling an item disguised as another legitimate item.

    4. No inappropriate names, skins, builds, or chat.

    Any names (IGN, nickname, tool name, or pet name) or other inappropriate behavior or text is not permitted.

    • This is not an exhaustive list, but examples of "inappropriate" include:
      • Referencing or promoting Nazi ideology, genocide, and discrimination against marginalized groups of people.
      • Themes that are sexually explicit in nature.

    5. Be respectful and kind to others. Treat everyone with the same level of respect you would want for yourself.

    Be respectful. We want everyone to have a fun time here.

    6. Do not use public channels to discuss real world politics, historical tragedies, or controversial public figures. Do not bring up illicit substances or other mature topics.

    This is a Minecraft server - a lot of people use it as a safe space to get away from real world stress. Be mindful of others when you bring up potentially triggering topics. If you insist on having these discussions, please do so in DMs or Party/Local chat.

    7. Predatory behavior will not be tolerated.

    This is self explanatory.

    8. Do not harass other players.

    Harassment can be identified as, but is not limited to the following:

    • Consistent unsolicited messaging (in-game, private messages, mail).
    • Unsolicited advertisements for items (in-game, private messages, mail) use trade chat.
    • Tp request spam.
    • Refusing to leave a build or claim.
    • Targeted profanity.

    9. Do not share personal information in public chats.

    While we discourage the sharing of personal information in general, if you insist on doing so, please keep it in private messages.

    • Twitter, streaming platforms, and generic personal information is allowed. Do not share information about other people without their consent.

    10. Staff impersonation is not permitted.

    Examples of this are changing your nickname or claiming in chat to be staff.

    11. Do not falsely report players.

    Please refrain from filing joke or slanderous reports on other players as this wastes staff's time and hurts others.

    12. Ban evasion is not permitted.

    Ban evasion is using an alt account to log in to the server to bypass an existing ban on another account.

    13. Do not circumvent the chat filter.

    If what you say is still being censored at the slurs level, it is not acceptable under any circumstance. Deliberately working around the chat filter will result in an immediate mute.

    14. Do not abuse alternative accounts.

    Alt abuse can be defined as, but is not limited to:

    • Looting the contents of accounts that do not belong to you (leaked accounts).

    26. The advertising of in game assets for IRLC is strictly prohibited in both public chats in game, and in our discord channels. Any external trading should be done at your own risk, and with the understanding that we cannot assist you.
